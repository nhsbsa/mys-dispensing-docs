Medication which will be covered by the HRT PPC

Not all medication will be covered by the HRT PPC. It's important to check that the medication they require will be eligible before purchasing this certificate.

If the medication they need is not listed here they are unable to purchase an HRT PPC, but will be able to purchase a PPC.

As other medication becomes licensed for the treatment of HRT, it may be added to this list.
