Sell a Prescription Prepayment Certificate (PPC)

You can help your patients save money on NHS prescriptions by selling them a PPC.

They can pay a set price for unlimited prescriptions for either 3 or 12 months.

The cost of prepaying is:

  • £29.65 for 3 months
  • £105.90 for 12 months

After payment, we can send the certificate details to your patient by email or text, or you can print them yourself.

Your patient needs to show their certificate details when they collect their prescription. They can use it straight away if they start it from today.
