Covid-19 Lateral Flow Device (LFD) Distribution Service

Use this service from 4 October 2021 to record your LFD delivery and collection information.

From this page you can:

  • enter essential details about the cartons of LFD test kits you have received
  • submit a claim for your daily LFD test kit collections

    • LFD deliveries - tell us about the cartons of LFD test kits you received

    We need you to enter lot details for cartons of LFD test kits you have had delivered to your premises.

    This is so any faulty test kits can be identified and recalled by the Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency (MHRA).

    For each delivery of cartons of test kits you receive, you'll need to record:

    • the date you received the lot
    • the wholesaler who supplied the lot
    • the lot number
    • the number of cartons of test kits delivered

LFD collections - make a daily claim for each time a patient or customer collects LFD test packs from your premises

For each collection, you'll need to record:

  • if the collection was made anonymously or not
  • the 16 digit collection code, if given
  • the lot numbers of test packs given out
  • the number of test packs given out
