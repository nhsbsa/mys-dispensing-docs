Sell a Prescription Prepayment Certificate (PPC)

Important: Important information:

If you only require HRT prescription drugs then an HRT PPC may be more beneficial for you.

You can help your customer save money on NHS prescriptions by selling them a PPC.

They can pay a set price for unlimited prescriptions for either 3 or 12 months.

The cost of a prepayment certificate is:

  • £30.25 for 3 months
  • £108.10 for 12 months

After payment, we can send the certificate details to your customer by email or post. You can also give them the details to use yourself.

Your customer needs to show their certificate details when they collect their prescription. They can use it straight away if they start it from today.

For more information visit Pharmacy sales - NHS Prescription Prepayment Certificate (PPC)
