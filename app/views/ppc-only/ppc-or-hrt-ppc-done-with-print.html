Collecting prescriptions

Tick box 'F' on the back of your prescription. TBC

Pharmacy staff ask for prepayment details. If you do not have them with you, they tick 'Evidence not seen' on the prescription. They will not charge you. Ask your pharmacist for help if you don't have evidence of your HRT PPC with you.

We prevent fraud by matching certificate details on our database with prescriptions. You are at risk of receiving a fine if you can not prove your HRT exemption if asked at a later date.
