Sell a Prescription Prepayment Certificate (PPC) or a Hormone Replacement Therapy Prescription Prepayment Certificate (HRT PPC)

Help your customer save money with their NHS prescriptions by selling them either a PPC or HRT PPC.

The customer can pay a set price for unlimited prescriptions

  • PPC: 3 or 12 months certificate
  • HRT PPC: 12 months only certificate

Questions and information for the customer, prior to sale

  • Check your customer hasn’t previously purchased a PPC before proceeding
  • Confirm which PPC is most suitable to the customer
  • Let the customer know they are not able to claim a refund for an HRT PPC
  • The customer can use their PPC straight away if they start it from today

The cost of a prepayment certificate

  • PPC: £30.25 for 3 months or £108.10 for 12 months
  • HRT PPC: £18.70 for 12 months

After payment, we can send the certificate details to your customer by email, by post – or you can give them the details.

Tell your customer that they are at risk of receiving a fine if they can not prove their exemption, if asked at a later date.

If the customer forgets their PCC, refer to the FP57 form to help them.

We do not send out plastic cards any more as they are costly to the NHS and the environment.
