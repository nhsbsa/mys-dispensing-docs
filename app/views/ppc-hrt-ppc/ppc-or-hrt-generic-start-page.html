Sell a PPC or an HRT PPC

Help your customer save money with their NHS prescriptions by selling them either a PPC or HRT PPC.

A PPC covers all the customer’s NHS prescriptions, an HRT PPC covers only the customer’s HRT prescriptions.

Customers can use their PPC or HRT PPC straight away, if they start it from today.

  • A PPC costs £30.25 for 3 months or £108.10 for 12 months
  • An HRT PPC costs £18.70 for 12 months (no 3 month option).

Check here to find out which HRT drugs are covered by the HRT PPC

Questions and information for customers

  • Check which PPC is most suitable for your customer
  • Let customers know they are not able to claim a refund for an HRT PPC
  • If a customer can't pay for their NHS prescription, you must still provide it to them
  • Tell customers that they are at risk of receiving a fine if they can not prove their exemption. Refer to the FP57 form if they’ve forgotten their certificate.

After payment, we can send the certificate details to your customer by email, by post – or you can give them the details.

We do not send out plastic cards as they are costly to the NHS and the environment.
